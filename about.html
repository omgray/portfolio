About Me

Photo of me:

I'm Ray, graphic designer and web developer residing in Daytona Beach, Fl.

I started my graphic design about ten years ago, working with various clients along the way. Starting out desinging flyers for local events, I would end up creating logos, brands and campaigns for various small businesses around the nation. It wasn't until 2018 when I decided delve into the world of software, starting with web development. As an experienced programmer, I am now able to offer my clients a whole new world of services! I am constantly working each day to further improve my skill and hone my abilities.
