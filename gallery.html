Villains *new!

Released: Fall 2021

Everyone loves a hero, but no doubt villains are cooler. Here are portraits of some of my all time favorite anime villains plus their alternate forms. Note the plain backgrounds for the initial forms and a unique backdrop for the second forms.

Freiza

Freiza's original final form from the Dragonball series. But this isn't even his final, final form!

See Full Size

Golden Freiza

Golden Freiza from the Dragonball Super era, complete with Dragonball style powerup aura. A very cool new form for a classic villain.

See Full Size

Madara Uchiha (Edo Tensei)

Madara Uchiha from the Naruto series. Edo Tensei, or reanimation, is Madara's initial form when he was formally introduced in Naruto Shippuden. This technique brings back the dead as a reanimated body.

See Full Size

Madara Uchiha (Rinne Tensei)

Later on in the story, Madara Uchiha is brought back to life completely using Rinne Tensei, or Rinne Rebirth, to change his reanimated body to a real, living body. The Susano'o ribcage was added for a cool effect.

See Full Size

Ulquiorra Cifer

Ulquiorra Cifer from the Bleach series. I didn't watch much Bleach growing up, but I was able to catch his fight against Ichigo.

See Full Size

Ulquiorra Cifer (Segunda Etapa)

Segunda Etapa roughly means resurrection: second stage in Spanish. This technique, which only Ulquiorra can do, changes himself back to the original form of his tribe.

See Full Size

Kaido

Kaido, from One Piece, is another villain I wasn't familiar with growing up. He's one of the four emporors of the world and, thus, one of the four most powerful characters in the series.

See Full Size

Kaido (Dragon Form)

Kaido can transform himself into a dragon! As one of the Beast Pirates, Kaido ate the Uo Uo No Me devil fruit, which gives him the ability to partially or completely turn into a dragon. He can also create fire clouds that he can use as platforms.

See Full Size

Tomura Shigaraki

From the My Hero Academia series, Tomura Shigaraki. Quirk: Decay. He can disintegrate anything he touches with all five fingers.

See Full Size

Plus Ultra Shigaraki

This version of Shigaraki has technically not appeared in the anime yet. He's become so powerful here that he's even decaying the background! Hopefully this is not too much of a spoiler.

See Full Size

Hero Hunter Garou

From One Punch Man, Garou the Hero Hunter comes through. Introduced in season 2 of One Punch Man, Garou has become a fan favorite.

See Full Size

Awakened Garou (Monster Form)

This version of Garou has not appeared in the anime yet. There was no reference to go off of as far as color and only a few manga panels for the actual character. He really only recently appeared, so this may not be how he looks once he debus in the anime.

Aback The World Turtle Series

Released: Spring 2018 / Summer 2020

This series explores a concept; what if the planet we lived on is actually a giant turtle? Aback The World Turtle depicts several locations in a fantasy style world with its own lore and story told through the images. There's a traveler in every picture, meant to be the point of immersion into the World Turtle as well as a showcase of scale. Capping the series is a world map with each location in relation to each other.

Magenta Plains

This piece depicts Magenta Plains. A traveler is depicted looking among the Plains on the turtles back. The turtles head is visible here.

See Full Size

Palisades Complex

This piece takes us to the Turtles right leg, with the rest of the leg visible in the distance. This location is called Palisades Complex.

See Full Size

Spiral Valley

This piece takes us to the Turtles tail, in an area called Spiral Valley. Pack a jacket, this valley environment is freezing cold!

See Full Size

Winter Hills

This is Winter Hills, the highest point on the Turtles Back. None of the Turtles features are visible here besides the endless mountain range.

See Full Size

Magic Sky

Located within a hollow region of the Turtles Shell lies a city called Magic Sky. Here, the traveler is looking toward the city from a local park.

See Full Size

Utsukushii Basin

Another location within the Hollow Shell region. Utsukushii Basin, depicted in this piece, is not too far from Magic Sky. At night, expect the local flora to glow different hues.

See Full Size

Yydra's Tongue

Inside the Mouth Region lies Yydra's Tongue, a massive cavelike area with plains and mountains in the distance.

See Full Size

Blight Mountain Range

Atop the Turtles head is the Blight Mountain Range. The magma here is blue and corrosively electric. Floating islands loom in the distance.

See Full Size

World Turtle Series World Map

This map of Yydra, the World Turtle, shows the various locations in the series relative to each other.
